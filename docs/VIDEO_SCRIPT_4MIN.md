# Recoup — 4-minute demo script (Game Bar: Win+Alt+R to start/stop)

> One take, live site only: https://recoup-agent-681822930558.us-central1.run.app
> Talk over the screen. Keep the cursor moving with your words. Numbers below are timestamps.

---

## 0:00–0:20 — THE HOOK (landing page on screen)
**Say:**
"Every budgeting app on the planet — Emma, Rocket Money, Snoop — does the same thing: it connects
your bank and shows you where your money WENT. Nobody gets it BACK.
**Budgeting apps show where your money went. Recoup gets it back.**
And it never touches your bank — it works from the receipts already sitting in your email."

*(Screen: hero — let the gold word-shine on "owed" play. Slow scroll past the trust box.)*

## 0:20–0:50 — SIGN IN = YOUR REAL DATA, INSTANTLY
**Do:** Click **Sign in** → Continue with Google → land in the command center with YOUR real
subscriptions already loaded.
**Say:**
"One tap. I sign in with Google and Recoup scans my real inbox in the same pass — read-only,
nothing sent, nothing deleted. These aren't demo numbers: these are MY actual subscriptions,
found from MY real receipts. And I can add a second inbox — it merges every mailbox into one
money surface, like an MCP over your email accounts."

## 0:50–1:40 — AUTOPILOT: THE AUTONOMOUS MISSION (the money shot)
**Do:** Click **⚡ Run Autopilot**. Let the 5 phases stream in. Point at each phase as you talk.
**Say:**
"Now watch the agent work — autonomously. Phase one: it scans my surface. Phase two: it grounds
every single claim in MongoDB Atlas — real vector search, you can see the similarity scores and
the actual laws: FTC Click-to-Cancel, EU261 flight compensation, the Fair Credit Billing Act.
Phase three: Gemini 3 through Google's Agent Development Kit drafts every claim. Phase four: an
independent verifier checks each one — and flags the ones I might not qualify for, instead of
hiding them. Phase five — and this is the whole product philosophy — it STOPS. Ten claims queued
at MY approve button. Recoup is autonomous everywhere except the one place it shouldn't be."

## 1:40–2:20 — FROM FINDING TO ACTION (cancel portal + tone + Gmail draft)
**Do:** Scroll to **Top Money Drains**. Click **Cancel** on the top drain → vendor's own cancel
page opens in a new tab → close it. Back: open a claim's **Show work** drawer → click tone
**Firm** → click **Draft in Gmail** (your compose window opens pre-filled) → close it.
**Say:**
"It ranks my biggest drains — cancel these three, keep this much per year. The cancel button
takes me to NETFLIX'S OWN cancellation page — not some fake portal. For claims, the agent shows
its full evidence pack: the rule, the verifier's checks, even why I might NOT qualify. I pick a
tone — polite, firm, or short — and one click drafts it in MY OWN Gmail. I press send. Money only
ever moves on the vendor's or government's official site."

## 2:20–3:00 — REAL GOVERNMENT MONEY (the proof moment)
**Do:** Scroll to **Search real unclaimed money** → type **GARCIA** → results appear.
**Say:**
"And here's the part you can verify yourself. Recoup indexes twenty thousand REAL records from
the official California State Controller — thirty-seven point eight MILLION dollars of real
unclaimed money. GARCIA: real person, four thousand dollars, held at Wells Fargo. Take this name
to claimit.ca.gov right now and you'll find the same record. Real data, and the claim happens
only on the official state site."

## 3:00–3:30 — TRUST ARCHITECTURE (chatbot + audit)
**Do:** Open the **✦ guide** → type "is my money safe?" → show the answer. Then click the audit
verify link (health JSON in a new tab, point at audit.intact + head hash).
**Say:**
"The built-in agent guides you — and it can drive the whole app. Every step the system takes is
written to a tamper-evident SHA-256 hash chain. Change one event and every later link breaks.
That's checkable trust — not a promise. When Gemini's free-tier quota runs hot, a Gemma
resilience tier takes over instantly and the UI says so honestly. The AI never invents an amount,
never claims something was sent, and never acts without me."

## 3:30–4:00 — CLOSE (back to landing or autopilot result)
**Say:**
"Gemini 3 reasoning through Google's ADK. The official MongoDB MCP server. Atlas Vector Search as
the agent's legal memory. Real government data. A real bank-CSV parser. And a human approval gate
the agent cannot cross.
Emma shows you where your money went. **Recoup gets it back.**
Recoup — recover what's yours."

*(Stop recording: Win+Alt+R)*
